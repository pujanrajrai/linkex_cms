About Us

WELCOME TO LINKEX EXPORT & LOGISTICS

Linkex Export & Logistics Pvt. Ltd. is an international cargo and courier company based in Kathmandu that provides complete logistics solutions with a customer first approach. Our mission is to make global shipping simple, safe and affordable for everyone. We specialize in exporting goods from Nepal while maintaining high quality service standards and promoting Nepali products in international markets.

With strong global partnerships, a growing network and years of expertise, Linkex export and logistic Pvt. Ltd is continuously expanding its reach to connect Nepal to the world through reliable and efficient logistics solutions.

Our Mission, Vision and Goal

Mission

To provide seamless, reliable, and efficient logistics solutions that connect Nepal to the world, ensuring customer satisfaction through innovative services and global partnerships.

Vision

To be Nepal's most trusted global logistics partner, recognized for excellence in international trade facilitation and innovative logistics solutions.

Our Goal

To deliver exceptional customer service, ensure on-time delivery, expand our network coverage globally, and continuously improve our logistics solutions.

Global Network

Personalized Service
