Who We Are

Linkex Export

Ship Global Nepal is a courier company established in 2014, headquartered in Kathmandu, Nepal. With a team of experts, our aim is to provide the best international and national courier services to our diverse clients at affordable prices. Each courier operates on a fixed timetable within an exclusive territory. Customers of Ship Global Nepal are encouraged to interact with their courier on a first-name basis, with full contact details and timetable scheduling provided.

OUR MISSION

To offer practical, cost-effective, and innovative freight management solutions through our ambitious team that comprises of our staff, management, and network partners.

OUR VISION

To be recognized as a quality operator in the domestic and global logistics arena by offering total logistics solutions to meet our customer demands, and work towards improving customer satisfaction, stable growth, and profitability.

ASSOCIATED WITH

We are happy to be part of the Courier Service Association of Nepal (COSAN).

Frequently Asked Questions

What type of goods do you accept for shipment?

We accept shipments such as general cargo, mail, perishable products, pharmaceuticals, valuable cargo, automotive parts and automobiles, odd size cargo among others which may require special handling procedures.

What goods are considered dangerous while transporting?

Dangerous goods are articles or substances which are capable of posing a hazard to health, safety, property or the environment and which are shown in the list of dangerous goods in IATA dangerous goods regulations or which are classified according to the IATA dangerous goods regulations.

How can I track my shipment?

You can track your shipment, provided that you have a tracking number issued by Linkex Export from here.
